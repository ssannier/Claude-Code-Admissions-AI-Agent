Mapua University Admissions Information

## About Mapua University
Mapua University, founded in 1925 by Don Tomas Mapua, is a world-class higher education institution in the Philippines. Recognized by the 2025 Times Higher Education World University Rankings, Mapua University is committed to providing a learning environment rooted in discipline, excellence, commitment, integrity, and relevance.

## Admission Requirements

### Undergraduate Applicants
- High school diploma or equivalent
- Minimum GPA requirements (vary by program)
- Entrance examination scores (Mapua Admission Test)
- Letters of recommendation (2 required)
- Personal statement or essay
- Valid ID and birth certificate

### Graduate Applicants
- Bachelor's degree from an accredited institution
- Minimum GPA of 2.5 (on a 4.0 scale)
- Graduate admission test scores (if applicable)
- Two letters of recommendation
- Statement of purpose
- Resume or curriculum vitae

### International Students
- All requirements for domestic students, plus:
- English proficiency test (TOEFL/IELTS)
- Evaluation of foreign credentials
- Student visa documentation
- Financial capability proof

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I apply to multiple programs?**
A: Yes, you can apply to up to 2 programs per application cycle.

**Q: Is housing available?**
A: Limited on-campus housing is available. Off-campus housing assistance is provided.

**Q: Can I transfer credits from another university?**
A: Yes, credits from accredited institutions can be evaluated for transfer. Contact the registrar's office.

**Q: Do you accept international students?**
A: Yes, we welcome international students from all countries.

**Q: What is the student-to-faculty ratio?**
A: Our student-to-faculty ratio is 15:1, ensuring personalized attention.

**Q: Are there part-time study options?**
A: Yes, many programs offer flexible scheduling for working students.

**Q: How do I schedule a meeting with an admissions advisor?**
A: You can request an advisor handoff through our AI chat system, or contact us directly at admissions@mapua.edu.ph.
